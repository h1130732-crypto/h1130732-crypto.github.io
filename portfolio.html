1. 全球留學戰略計畫 (ABC 計畫)

1. Global Strategy & Target Schools

*學費方針：需自行負擔或透過獎學金/助學貸款支付。

*Tuition Policy: Self-funding required, targeting scholarships or student loans.

【方案 A】美國 (United States)

【Plan A】United States

目標學校：Cornell, Yale, UPenn, Dartmouth, UC Berkeley, UCLA, UCSD, Georgetown, UMich, NYU

Targets: Cornell, Yale, UPenn, Dartmouth, UC Berkeley, UCLA, UCSD, Georgetown, UMich, NYU

  • 優勢：最頂尖的產品經理 (PM)、創投、AI 與科技生態圈；跨學門彈性極大。
  • 劣勢：學費極其昂貴；H-1B 簽證抽籤具備高度不確定性。
  • Pros: Ideal ecosystem for PM, tech, AI, and startups. Academic flexibility.
  • Cons: Extremely expensive; H-1B visa uncertainty.

【方案 B】加拿大 / 新加坡 / 香港

【Plan B】Canada / SG / HK

目標學校：Waterloo, UTo, McGill, UBC / NUS, NTU / HKU, HKUST

Targets: Waterloo, Toronto, McGill, UBC / NUS, NTU / HKU, HKUST

  • 優勢：滑鐵盧實習系統 (Co-op) 強大；學費較低且移民政策相對友善。
  • 劣勢：文化更偏向純工程領域；薪資天花板通常比美國低。
  • Pros: Highly recognized tech Co-op (Waterloo); easier immigration pathways.
  • Cons: Engineering-focused tech cultures; slightly lower salaries.

【方案 C】台灣不分系 (Taiwan Open Curriculum)

【Plan C】Taiwan Universities (Undecided Major)

  • 優勢：成本最低；擁有極致自由度來設計個人課表、營運個人新創、YouTube 頻道與模聯活動。
  • 劣勢：全球品牌效應相較於美加名校較弱；科技業產品經理 (PM) 的直接管道非常有限。
  • Pros: Lowest cost, ultimate freedom to pursue side projects, startups, YouTube, and MUN.
  • Cons: Weaker global brand; limited pipeline for PM recruiting compared to the US.

3. 申請文書故事線 (Essays & Personal Statement)

3. Application Narrative & Essay Blueprint

核心敘事核心 (The Narrative Voice)

The Narrative Voice

拒絕流水帳，將所有的申請文書（Essay）聚焦於我「個人的獨特故事與真實聲音」。透過媒體（YouTube/Podcast）的主持感染力，結合外交（ICDF/外交小尖兵）的街頭實踐，向主審官傳遞一位具備理性談判能力、又有感性敘事溫度的青年領袖形象。

Reject typical formats. Focus on individual story and personal voice. Combine media communication with global diplomacy initiatives to project a rational yet highly empathetic changemaker.

目前正全力衝刺的剩餘項目 (What is Left to Do)

Ongoing Action Items

  • 活動強化：持續拉高核心課外活動的影響力與實質成果，以建構無懈可擊的主故事線。
  • Extracurriculars: Solidifying milestone outcomes to complete the narrative.
  • 標準化考試：全面衝刺 SAT 與 Duolingo English Test (DET) 的極致高分。
  • Standardized Tests: Maximizing SAT and Duolingo English Test performance.

4. 擬定主修科系與未來職涯對照

4. Target Majors & Prospective Careers

1. 國際關係 (International Relations)

1. International Relations

專注領域：全球政治、現代外交、國際合作與衝突解決、國家安全方針。

Focus: Global politics, modern diplomacy, international conflict resolution.

🎯 理想職涯：外交官 (Diplomat)、政策分析師、國際戰略顧問

🎯 Careers: Diplomat, Policy Analyst, International Consultant

2. 傳播學 (Communication)

2. Communication

專注領域：公眾演說與說服藝術、媒體策略、戰略性訊息傳遞、組織溝通學。

Focus: Public speaking, persuasion mechanics, media and strategic messaging.

🎯 理想職涯：公關經理 (PR Manager)、行銷策略師、企業傳播專家

🎯 Careers: Public Relations Manager, Marketing Strategist

3. 政治學 (Political Science)

3. Political Science

專注領域：政府體制、公共政策模型剖析、政治行為與全球選舉戰略。

Focus: Government frameworks, public policy analysis, election dynamics.

🎯 理想職涯：政治顧問、政府體系官員、智庫政策學者

🎯 Careers: Political Consultant, Government Official

4. 媒體研究 (Media Studies)

4. Media Studies

專注領域：大眾媒體對社會之衝擊、數位平台演算法、文化與科技傳播交織。

Focus: Media's social impacts, digital platform growth, and technology.

🎯 理想職涯：媒體戰略總監、數位內容製作人、社群平台經理

🎯 Careers: Media Strategist, Content Producer, Social Media Manager
