影視鏡頭下的國際視角

Global Perspectives via Media

從《開拍吧孩子！》全國冠軍到主持第二季，影音是我與世界對話的方式。我創立 YouTube 頻道分析阿富汗受教權等國際時事，將雙語能力化為實質影響力。

From a national championship in elementary school to hosting major youth programs, filmmaking is my voice. I analyze international affairs on my YouTube channel, turning bilingual skills into social impact.

思辨交鋒：辯論與模聯

The Art of Debate & MUN

在 NHSDLC 與 YMUNT 耶魯模聯中，我與各國精英唇槍舌戰，這不僅訓練了邏輯，更讓我深刻理解國際政治的厚度。我亦創立 Wego ICDF 社團，將愛心募款帶往帛琉。

Through intense exchanges at YMUNT and NHSDLC, I have honed my logical reasoning. I founded the Wego ICDF club to practice real-world diplomacy, bringing aid to primary schools in Palau.

My visit to the U.S. as a Diplomatic Envoy was a turning point. Recently, I launched a Podcast to build bridges between generations and share the powerful stories of our time.
